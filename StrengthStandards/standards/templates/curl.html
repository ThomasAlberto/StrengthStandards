What do the strength standards mean?

Beginner You are like baby!
Novice Stronger than 20% of lifters. You've probably been training up to six months.
Intermediate Stronger than 50% of lifters. You've probably been training for up to two years with good nutrition and effort.
Advanced Stronger than 80% of lifters. You've probably been training for five years with excellent effort, nutrition, and recuperation.
Elite Stronger than 99% of lifters. You have completed the gym and it is finally time to start real life.
